[ N°003 / Approach ]
The process
Every project follows the same rigorous arc — from research and concept to shaders and shipping.
PHASE 01
Research & concept
Deep dives into your brand, audience, and category. References, mood, narrative arc. The thinking before the making.
PHASE 02
Design & motion
Visual systems, type, color, and the choreography of every transition. Built to feel inevitable, not decorated.
PHASE 03
Build & ship
Three.js, GSAP, GLSL shaders, and modern frameworks. Performance is a creative constraint, not an afterthought.
[ N°004 / About ]
The studio
I'm an independent creative developer based in Berlin, working at the intersection of code, motion, and 3D. For seven years I've built digital experiences for brands, artists, and studios — from interactive product launches to award-winning portfolio sites. My focus is on the small details: timing, easing, the weight of a font, the way a transition lands.
